How to Measure Yourself

Chest / Bust

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape measure level and snug but not tight.

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline — the narrowest part of your torso, usually just above the navel.

Hips

Stand with feet together and measure around the widest part of your hips and buttocks.

Foot Length

Stand on a piece of paper, mark the heel and longest toe, then measure the distance in centimetres.
