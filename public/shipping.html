Return Policy

We want you to love your purchase. If something isn't right, we're here to help. Returns and exchanges are accepted within 7 days of delivery.

Conditions for Returns

  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and undamaged with all original tags and packaging intact.
  • Proof of purchase (order confirmation or receipt) is required.
  • Items purchased during clearance sales are final and non-refundable unless defective.
  • Underwear, swimwear, and personalised items are not eligible for return due to hygiene reasons.

Exchange Process

1

Contact Us

Reach out via WhatsApp or email within 7 days of delivery with your order number and reason.

2

Ship It Back

Send the item back to our Lagos store. Return shipping costs are your responsibility unless the item is faulty.

3

Get Your Exchange

Once we receive and inspect the item, we'll ship out the replacement within 2 business days.

Refund Timeline
